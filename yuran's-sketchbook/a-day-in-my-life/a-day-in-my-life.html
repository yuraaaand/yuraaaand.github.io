a day in my life.



A Day In My Life is an interactive web experience that asks our users to reflect on their desires in their future design careers. Working with the latest 'AIGA Design Census' data to create an interactive communication system, it presents a series of illustrations that form a story of a day in the imagined life of a designer, and prompts the users to make a selection between two metaphoric representations based on their values. This is a collaborative project with Alice Cai and Taery Kim, mentored by Kyuha(Q) Shim. I helped with visual identity, UI and UX, data analysis, and was responsible for web-development.
